Methodology

Comparable products first

BasketGuard compares products inside defined equivalence groups, not broad supermarket categories.

Unit price is the anchor

Shelf price, pack size and unit basis stay visible so large packs and loyalty pricing do not hide the comparison.

Scores need evidence

Offender scores combine retailer-specific movement and current premium, then show the evidence behind the verdict.

Caveats stay visible

Missing, stale or low-confidence data should reduce certainty instead of becoming a stronger claim.
